    1. Data analyst, Kaggle Dog Breed Identification and Prediction Competition

      • In this project, I have built a multi class image classifier using Transfer learning and TensorFlow 2.0 to identify different breeds of dogs from images. Data is provided by the Kaggle Dog Breed Identification Competition. It consists of a collection of 20,000+ images (labelled and not labelled) of 120 different dog breeds.
      • The aim is to create a classifier capable of predicting a dog's breed from a photo.
